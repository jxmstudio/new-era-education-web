Art Workshop Schedule & Structure

Our art workshops are designed to inspire creativity while developing essential skills. Each session focuses on different techniques and projects, ensuring a well-rounded artistic education.

Session Times

After-school and weekend workshops available. Each session runs for 1.5 hours with age-appropriate activities.

Small Group Learning

Maximum of 8 children per workshop to ensure individual attention and creative space for each artist.

Skill Development

Progressive skill building with projects that challenge and develop artistic abilities over time.

Flexible Enrollment

Drop-in sessions, term programs, or holiday intensives available to suit your family's schedule.
