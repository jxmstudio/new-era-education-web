Maths Tutoring Schedule & Structure

Our Maths tutoring sessions are designed to build confidence and mathematical thinking progressively. Each session combines conceptual understanding with practical problem-solving activities.

Session Times

After-school and weekend sessions available. Each session runs for 1 hour with flexible scheduling.

Individual & Small Groups

One-on-one tutoring or small group sessions (maximum 4 students) for personalised attention.

Progress Assessment

Regular assessments and detailed progress reports to track mathematical development and identify areas for focus.

Flexible Programs

Short-term intensive programs or ongoing weekly sessions to suit your child's mathematical needs and schedule.
