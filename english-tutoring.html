English Tutoring Schedule & Structure

Our English tutoring sessions are designed to build confidence and improve skills progressively. Each session focuses on specific learning objectives while maintaining engagement and enjoyment.

Session Times

After-school and weekend sessions available. Each session runs for 1 hour with flexible scheduling.

Individual & Small Groups

One-on-one tutoring or small group sessions (maximum 4 students) for personalized attention.

Progress Assessment

Regular assessments and detailed progress reports to track improvement and identify areas for focus.

Flexible Programs

Short-term intensive programs or ongoing weekly sessions to suit your child's needs and schedule.
