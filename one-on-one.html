Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tutoring services

What subjects do you cover?

We cover all core subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, and Humanities. Adelaide specializes in literacy development, numeracy skills, and VCE exam preparation. We can also help with study skills, essay writing, and exam techniques.

How long are the tutoring sessions?

Sessions are available in 30-minute, 1-hour, or 1.5-hour durations. We recommend 1-hour sessions for most students as they provide enough time for meaningful learning while maintaining focus. The duration can be adjusted based on your child's age and attention span.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online tutoring sessions. Online sessions use interactive digital platforms with screen sharing, digital resources, and real-time collaboration tools. Both formats provide the same personalized attention and learning outcomes.

What are your rates?

Our rates vary based on session duration and format. We offer competitive pricing for our qualified teaching services. Please contact us for current rates and any available package discounts. We also offer a free initial consultation to discuss your child's needs.

How do you track my child's progress?

We provide regular progress updates including weekly summaries and monthly detailed reports. These include assessment results, goal achievements, and recommendations for continued improvement. Parents receive comprehensive feedback on their child's development.
