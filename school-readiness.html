Program Schedule & Structure

Our School Readiness Program runs throughout the year with flexible scheduling options to accommodate your family's needs. Each session is carefully designed to balance learning with fun, ensuring your child develops a love for learning.

Session Times

Morning and afternoon sessions available Monday to Saturday. Each session runs for 1.5 hours.

Small Group Learning

Maximum of 6 children per group to ensure individual attention and personalized learning.

Progress Tracking

Regular assessments and detailed progress reports to keep parents informed of their child's development.

Flexible Enrollment

Enroll anytime throughout the year. Programs available for 3-month, 6-month, or full-year terms.
