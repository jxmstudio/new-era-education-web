Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from parents and students.

What ages do you cater for?

We offer tutoring services for students from ages 3 to 18. Our programs include:

  • School Readiness Program: Ages 3-5
  • Primary School Tutoring: Foundation to Year 6
  • Secondary School Tutoring: Years 7-12
  • One-on-One Tutoring: All ages
  • Art Workshops: All ages

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online tutoring options. Our online sessions use interactive smartboards and engaging digital tools to ensure effective learning. Students can choose:

  • In-person sessions at our Mickleham learning centre
  • Online sessions via secure video conferencing
  • Hybrid approach combining both options

How do bookings work?

Booking a session is simple and straightforward:

  1. Initial Contact: Contact us via phone, email, or our online booking form
  2. Consultation: We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your child's needs
  3. Assessment: We conduct a learning assessment to understand your child's current level
  4. Custom Plan: We create a personalized learning plan
  5. Regular Sessions: Schedule regular tutoring sessions that work for your family

What's your cancellation policy?

We understand that life can be unpredictable. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair for both families and our tutors:

  • 24-hour notice: Free cancellation with 24 hours' notice
  • Same-day cancellation: 50% charge for same-day cancellations
  • No-show: Full session charge for no-shows
  • Emergency situations: We're flexible for genuine emergencies
  • Rescheduling: We'll work with you to reschedule when possible

What qualifications do your tutors have?

All our tutors are highly qualified and experienced:

  • Teaching qualifications from recognized institutions
  • Minimum 10 years of classroom experience
  • Specialized training in evidence-based teaching methods
  • Working with Children checks
  • Ongoing professional development

How do you track student progress?

We use a comprehensive approach to track and report student progress:

  • Weekly Updates: Parents receive weekly progress reports
  • Regular Assessments: Ongoing assessments to measure improvement
  • Goal Setting: Clear learning goals established with each student
  • Parent Meetings: Regular parent-teacher meetings
  • Digital Portfolio: Online access to student work and progress

What subjects do you offer?

We offer comprehensive tutoring in the following subjects:

  • English: Reading, writing, comprehension, creative writing, essay writing, VCE preparation
  • Mathematics: Foundation to Year 8 (Year 9-12 coming soon)
  • School Readiness: Literacy, numeracy, social skills, fine motor development
  • Art & Creativity: Creative expression, art appreciation, fine motor skills
  • Study Skills: Time management, organization, exam preparation

Do you offer group sessions or only one-on-one?

We offer both individual and small group sessions:

  • One-on-One Tutoring: Personalized attention for individual needs
  • Small Group Sessions: 2-4 students for collaborative learning
  • Art Workshops: Group sessions for creative activities
  • School Readiness Program: Small groups to develop social skills
  • Holiday Programs: Group activities during school holidays

What are your operating hours?

We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate busy families:

  • Monday: 8:00 AM - 7:30 PM
  • Tuesday: 8:00 AM - 7:30 PM
  • Wednesday: 8:00 AM - 7:30 PM
  • Thursday: Closed
  • Friday: Closed
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed
  • Online Sessions: Extended hours available
  • Holiday Programs: Special schedules during school holidays

How much do sessions cost?

Our pricing is competitive and transparent. Costs vary based on:

  • Session Type: One-on-one vs. group sessions
  • Duration: 30-minute, 45-minute, or 60-minute sessions
  • Frequency: Weekly, fortnightly, or casual bookings
  • Service Type: Different rates for different programs

Contact us for detailed pricing information and to discuss the best option for your family.
